About Me

I spend my time between two different worlds — building software and working behind a camera. One’s all about structure and systems, the other’s about instinct and storytelling. I’ve never really bought into the idea that you have to be just one thing. The stuff I do — whether it’s writing code or framing a shot — comes from the same place: wanting to make things that are clear, useful, and maybe even a little beautiful.
